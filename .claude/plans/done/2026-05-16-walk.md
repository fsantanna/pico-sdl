## Diverged from the original sketch

-   **Recursive, not iterative.** No `chain[64]`. The C stack IS the chain.
    `_xxx_root_fr` recurses up to root, then projects down on the unwind.
-   **Symmetric signatures.** Both halves take `(L, val, *out) â†’ Pico_Layer*`.
    The boundary `Pico_Rel_*` â†” `SDL_F*` conversion happens once at the
    public API.
-   **Assert outside.** `_cv_walk_*` captures both roots and asserts
    `R_fr == R_to` in the composed caller.
-   **No same-layer fast path.** Round-trip is mathematically identity
    (modulo float precision); the simpler control flow won out.
-   **Inlined `_cv_walk_*` into `pico_cv_*`.** Static helpers were only
    called once each.
-   **`_root_pos` / `_root_rect` NOT rewired.** Kept as thin
    `pico_cv_*` wrappers â€” single source of truth, minimal diff.
    The no-op down-recursion is one cheap base-case call.
